Mode 1 · Step 1

Register a Requirement

The initiator only enters business facts. The portal automatically fills initiation date, reference ID, Compliance Manager, Security Manager, Finance BP, and most Budget Owner assignments.

Automatic assignments

For Client / Supplier, the budget owner shall be the business unit primarily responsible for managing the client or supplier account.

Mode 1 · Step 2

Risk Assessment

Compliance Manager, Security Manager, and collaborators can add one or more risks against the selected requirement. The form captures title, entry date, inputter, description, negative consequences, evidence, and SLU score.
