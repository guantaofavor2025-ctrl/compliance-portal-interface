Design principle

Role-based navigation and permission model

The production system should authenticate each user, identify their roles, retrieve only matters where they are a participant or authorised viewer, and then route the user to the actions awaiting their input. The static prototype demonstrates the intended user experience only; it is not a security model.

General CounselCreates users, assigns roles, monitors all matters, and handles escalations or final approvals.
Compliance ManagerOwns assigned matters, completes risk assessment, prepares treatment decisions, and coordinates evidence.
ApproverReviews decision points and implementation reports assigned to them.
Finance / Budget OwnerReviews cost, ownership, and implementation responsibilities when a matter requires treatment.
Business RequesterSubmits requirements, responds to clarification requests, and tracks advice or program status.
